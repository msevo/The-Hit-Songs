Jive is a digital platform that connects musicians with an audience and helps artists showcase their work in an intimate environment. Jive will act as a digital venue, allowing artists to live stream their performances, accumulate “listeners”, and receive live feedback and donations for their performances. Our goal is to create a community of musicians and followers that places the music industry’s emphasis back on the artist.
